Envi-Projects

Envi-Projects

Our planet is changing. We need to help it change for the better and we're asking for your help to do that!

There are a lot of things that affect our planet in a bad way but the good news is that everyone can help to reduce them and do their bit for the environment.

Here's a list of some activities you can partake in!
Tree Planting!

Our goal is to plant a billion trees across the planet to slow the crisis of climate change.

Recycle!

Recycling community waste is a big step on the road to sustainability for your immediate environment.

Bike To Work Day!

The observance encourages us to bike to work, but it also raises awareness of cyclists.

Coastal Cleanup!

Coastal Cleanups engages people to remove trash from the world’s beaches and waterways.

Eco-LIVErary

Everyone has a story to tell.

Just like in any library, readers can browse this catalogue, and decide which “book” to “read” and “borrow” for an hour at a time. The only difference is that in the Eco-LIVErary BOOKS, books are people, and reading means listening to the stories of these random people about their environmental awareness journey.
